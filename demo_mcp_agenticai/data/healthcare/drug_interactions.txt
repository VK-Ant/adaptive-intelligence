Critical Drug Interactions Database: Metformin + IV contrast dye = risk of lactic acidosis, hold Metformin 48 hours before and after procedure. SGLT2 inhibitors + loop diuretics (Furosemide) = increased dehydration risk, monitor fluid intake and blood pressure. ACE inhibitors (Lisinopril) + potassium supplements = hyperkalemia risk, monitor serum potassium. Warfarin + NSAIDs (Ibuprofen) = increased bleeding risk, avoid combination. Metformin + alcohol = increased lactic acidosis risk. Empagliflozin + insulin = hypoglycemia risk, may need insulin dose reduction.
