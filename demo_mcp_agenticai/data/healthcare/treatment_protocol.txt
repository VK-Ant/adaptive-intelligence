Treatment Protocol 2025: Type 2 Diabetes management. First-line therapy: Metformin 500mg twice daily, titrate to 1000mg if tolerated. If HbA1c remains above 7.5% after 3 months, add SGLT2 inhibitor (Empagliflozin 10mg daily). Monitor kidney function (eGFR) every 6 months. Contraindicated if eGFR below 30. Common side effects: GI disturbance with Metformin, UTI risk with SGLT2 inhibitors. Annual eye exam required. Lifestyle modifications: 150 min moderate exercise weekly, carbohydrate counting, weight management target BMI under 30.
