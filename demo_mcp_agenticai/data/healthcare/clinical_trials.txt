Clinical Trial NCT-2025-0847: Empagliflozin Cardiovascular Outcomes Study. N=4200 patients, 36-month follow-up. Primary endpoint: MACE (major adverse cardiovascular events) reduction 23% vs placebo (p=0.003). Secondary endpoints: hospitalization for heart failure reduced 35% (p<0.001), all-cause mortality reduced 12% (p=0.08, not significant). Subgroup analysis: patients with eGFR 30-60 showed 28% MACE reduction. Adverse events: genital mycotic infections 8% vs 2% placebo, diabetic ketoacidosis 0.3% vs 0.1% placebo. Conclusion: Empagliflozin provides significant cardiovascular benefit with acceptable safety profile.
